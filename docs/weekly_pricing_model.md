Weekly Premium Model

The premium is calculated based on three main factors:

1 Risk of disruption in the delivery zone
2 Average weekly income of the worker
3 Historical weather patterns

Example Pricing

Low risk zone: ₹20 per week
Medium risk zone: ₹35 per week
High risk zone: ₹50 per week

Coverage

If a disruption causes loss of income, the system compensates the worker up to ₹2000 per week depending on the selected policy.
