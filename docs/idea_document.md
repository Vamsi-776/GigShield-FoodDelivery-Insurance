Problem Statement

Food delivery workers often lose income due to extreme weather conditions such as heavy rain, floods or severe pollution.

These disruptions reduce working hours and delivery opportunities.

Persona

Food delivery partners working for platforms like Swiggy and Zomato.

Solution

GigShield AI provides parametric insurance coverage for delivery workers.

Instead of manual claims, external triggers such as weather data automatically initiate compensation payouts.

Example

If rainfall exceeds a threshold and delivery activity drops significantly, the system automatically calculates income loss and provides compensation.
